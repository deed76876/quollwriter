<p class="help">Welcome to the Editors Service.  The {contacts} sidebar should currently be visible, you'll use it for managing your {contacts} and sending messages to them.</p>
<p class="help"><a href='qw:user-guide/editors/getting-started.html'>Click here for more detailed information about getting started.</a></p>
<h1 class="help">Your {contacts} list</h1>
<p class="help">The currently visible tab shows your {contacts}.  It will show all the {contacts} you have invited and/or have invited you.  This tab is always displayed.  You don't have any {contacts} at the moment but that's ok!</p>
<h1 class="help">Going online</h1>
<p class="help">To go online (i.e. to login to the Editors Service) just click on the&nbsp;&nbsp;[status-offline]&nbsp;&nbsp;button at the top of the sidebar.</p>
<p class="help">Once you're online you can click the button again to change your status, for example when you are writing you might want to set your status as&nbsp;&nbsp;[status-busy]<b>Busy</b>&nbsp;&nbsp;so people know not to bug you.</p>
<h1 class="help">Sending an invite</h1>
<p class="help">To send an invite all you need is their email address.  Click on the&nbsp;&nbsp;[add]&nbsp;&nbsp;button at the top of the sidebar to get started.  If the person already has an Editors Service account then you can send them your {project} straight away.</p>
<h1 class="help">Sending {an project}/{chapters}</h1>
<p class="help">Once you have sent the invite you can send your current {project} to the {editor}.  You can select which {chapters} are sent.  You can send {an project} at any time.  Just right click on the {editor} information and select <b>Send {project}/{chapters}</b>.</p>
<h1 class="help">Viewing {a contact}</h1>
<p class="help">To view {a contact} just click on their name/email address, the {contacts} sidebar will be displayed and a tab shown for the {contact}.  The tab will show all the chat messages you have exchanged.  There will also be a text box at the bottom of the tab that allows you to send a message to them.</p>
<p class="help">Right clicking on {a contact} will show you a menu of options.</p>
<h1 class="help">{Project} {editors}</h1>
<p class="help">Once you have sent your {project} to a {contact} they become a {project} {editor} and will be displayed under the <b>{Editors}</b> section of the {project} sidebar.  From there you can access any comments they have sent you about your {project}.</p>
